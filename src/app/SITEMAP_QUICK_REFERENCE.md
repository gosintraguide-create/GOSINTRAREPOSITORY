## ✅ What You Need to Do

### Step 1: Verify Files Are Live (2 minutes)
Visit these URLs in your browser:
- https://www.hoponsintra.com/robots.txt ← Should show robots.txt content
- https://www.hoponsintra.com/sitemap.xml ← Should show all 17 URLs

### Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console (5 minutes)
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Select your property (hoponsintra.com)
3. Click "Sitemaps" in left sidebar
4. Enter: `https://www.hoponsintra.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Click "Submit"

### Step 3: Request Indexing for Key Pages (10 minutes)
1. Go to "URL Inspection" in Search Console
2. Enter each URL and click "Request Indexing":
   - `/attractions/pena-palace`
   - `/attractions/quinta-da-regaleira`
   - `/attractions/moorish-castle`
   - `/blog/how-to-get-to-sintra`
   - `/blog/planning-your-perfect-day`

### Step 4: Wait and Monitor (Ongoing)
- **Week 1-2:** Google discovers pages
- **Week 2-4:** Pages start getting indexed
- **Week 4-8:** Traffic starts increasing
